\subsubsection{Linear Model}\label{linear-model}

In this problem a linear model \(y = ax+b\) is fit, where \(y\) is
Average Revenue and \(x\) is Hours of operation. The strategy was to set
\(a\) and \(b\) to be \emph{decision variables} and the \emph{minimize
the mean absolute deviation} between the fitted model and the actual
average revenue.

The \emph{LSRG} solver in the educational license of ASPE was not
available and hence a default non-linear optimization engine was chosen
with multiple start points.
